Frontiers await. Heed the call!
WAR ROBOTS: FRONTIERS is a new chapter in War Robots history. It’s a dynamic third person multiplayer shooter made with the power of Unreal Engine 5 for PC* and consoles. The game is set 200 years in the future of War Robots’ world, where players battle using giant robots.

Matches are 6v6 and are set in the destructible environment, so use it to your advantage. And if things get really tough, you can always call in the giant Titan to try and turn the tide. * The game is currently in the Early Access

Highly detailed and customizable robots for any play style

Matches with friends — you are never alone on the battlefield
